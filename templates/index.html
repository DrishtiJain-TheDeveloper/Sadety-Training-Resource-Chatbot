About SafetyTrainPro

SafetyTrainPro is a resource hub created to make safety training simple, accessible, and practical for everyone in the workplace.

Our goal is to help workers, managers, and employers understand essential safety requirements, stay up to date with compliance guidelines, and access reliable training materials that suit their specific roles and industries.

Whether you're just starting a job that requires safety certification, organizing a training program for your team, or looking to refresh your own knowledge, SafetyTrainPro brings together the information and tools you need — all in one place.This platform is built to support informed decision-making, promote safe work environments, and make safety training less overwhelming and more effective.

What our assistant can help with:

  • Finding training resources
  • Understanding regulatory requirements
  • Recommending role-based training paths
  • Explaining training standards
  • Safety training best practices
  • Creating training checklists
